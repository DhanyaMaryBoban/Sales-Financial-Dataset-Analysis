# 📊 Sales Financial Analysis with Power BI

**📌 Project Overview**
- This project explores a sales financial dataset using Power BI to uncover key business insights. The dataset includes:
  - Order Date
  - Customer Name
  - Territory
  - Product Line
  - Revenue
  - Profit
  - Year, Month, Quarter

- The analysis focuses on KPIs, top customers, territory efficiency, product growth trends, and revenue–profit correlation.

  **🛠 Tool Used**
Power BI for data visualization and dashboard creation.

 **🔑 Key Questions Answered**
- KPIs: Total revenue, total profit, and profit margin.
- Top Customers: Who are the top 10 customers by revenue?
- Territory Margins: How efficient are different regions?
- Product Growth: Which product lines are growing or declining?
- Correlation: Relationship between revenue and profit.

 **📈 Insights**
- Overall Profitability: Revenue of 13.97K with a healthy 30% profit margin.
- Top Customers: Tyrell Corp contributed 15.95% of total revenue.
- Territory Efficiency: Asia, North America, and Europe all achieved consistent 30% margins.
- Product Growth Trend: Electronics grew +60% between Jan–Aug 2023, while Furniture declined -28.33%.
- Correlation Analysis: Strong link between revenue and profit, with Electronics leading in both.

 **📌 Conclusion**
- This project demonstrates how Power BI can transform raw sales data into actionable insights, highlighting profitability, customer concentration, and product performance trends.
